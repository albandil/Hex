This program computes the T-matrix from the basic second order Born approximation,
\[ T = -4\pi^2 \left<\Psi_f\right| V + VGV \left|\Psi_i\right> \ . \]
The initial and final states \( \Psi \) are taken as products of the atomic (\(\psi\)) and projectile (\(\beta\)) function. The exchange interaction is ignored in the present implementation, as it doesn't influence the cross sections in the domain of validity of the method.
For details see the Doxygen documentation.
