This program computes the T-matrix from the basic first order Born approximation,
\[ T = -4\pi^2 \left<\Psi_f\right| H_{\mathrm{full}} - H_{\mathrm{free}} \left|\Psi_i\right> \ . \]
The initial and final states \( \Psi \) are taken as products of the atomic (\(\psi\)) and projectile (\(\beta\)) function. The exchange is accounted for by the (anti)symmetrization
\[ \Psi(1,2) = \psi(1)\beta(2) + (-1)^S \beta(1)\psi(2) \ , \]
depending on the total spin.
For details see the Doxygen documentation.
